Our Story

Stafford's Original
Full-Service Car Wash

610 Carwash has been a fixture on Garrisonville Road since 1988. For over 35 years, we have been the car wash Stafford trusts. We are not a chain. We are not a franchise. We are a locally owned business that takes pride in every vehicle that rolls through our bay.

Our brushless wash technology is gentle on your paint while tough on dirt. Every wash is finished by hand. We treat your vehicle the way we treat our own. From a quick exterior rinse to a full interior detail, we do it right every time.

What We Do

Full-Service Car Care

From a quick wash to a complete restoration. Every service, every time, done right.

Brushless Car Wash
Our signature brushless wash system is gentle on your finish and tough on grime. No scratches, no swirl marks. Just a clean, protected vehicle.
Interior Detailing
Deep cleaning, shampooing, and conditioning for your cabin. We restore seats, carpets, dashboard, and every surface until it looks and smells like new.
Exterior Detailing
Hand wash, clay bar, polish, and wax. We bring back the factory shine. Bug removal, tar removal, and paint protection included in every detail.
Tire & Wheel Cleaning
Brake dust, road grime, and curb stains removed. We clean and dress your tires and wheels until they shine like the day you bought them.
Vacuuming & Windows
Complete interior vacuum with crevice tool. Crystal clear windows inside and out. Included with every full-service wash or available standalone.
Ozone Treatment
Eliminate stubborn odors from smoke, pets, food, and mildew. Our ozone generator neutralizes bacteria and leaves your cabin fresh at the molecular level.

How It Works

From Dirty to Done

Drive in, relax, drive out clean. That simple.

Step 01
Pull In
Drive into our bay at 389 Garrisonville Rd. Our team greets you, walks around your vehicle, and helps you choose the right service for today.
Step 02
We Go to Work
Our brushless system cleans your exterior without touching your paint. For full-service and detail packages, our team handles the interior at the same time.
Step 03
Hand Finish
Every vehicle gets a hand dry, window wipe, and tire dressing. We inspect the results before you do. Nothing leaves unless it meets our standard.
Step 04
Drive Out Clean
Pay with any method you prefer. Roll out with a vehicle that looks, feels, and smells like the day you bought it. See you next time.
