Web App (PWA)
Included in Essential + Growth

A Progressive Web App runs inside the phone's browser but looks and feels like a real app. Your customers visit your website, tap "Add to Home Screen," and it installs instantly. No App Store download required.

Installs in 2 seconds from your website. No app store needed.
Works on both iPhone and Android phones.

No Apple/Google approval process. Goes live the same day.

Customer Retention Engine

How the Loyalty Points System Works

Available in the Growth and Premium packages. A points-based rewards program that turns one-time visitors into repeat customers who come back every week.

Think Wawa Rewards. Same concept. Wawa built a billion-dollar loyalty program where customers earn points on every purchase and redeem them for free items. Their program increased visit frequency by 35% and average ticket by 22%. We're building the same system, scaled for your car wash.

Customer Earns Points

Every time a customer pays for a wash or a service, they earn points automatically. For example: Basic Wash = 10 pts, Premium Wash = 25 pts, Full Detail = 50 pts. Points are tracked in the app.

Points Stack Up

Customers see their point balance in the app. It creates a "savings account" feeling. They think, "I have 80 points, just 20 more and I get a free wash." That's what drives them to come back instead of going to a competitor.

Redeem for Rewards

You decide what rewards to offer. 100 pts = Free Basic Wash. 200 pts = Free Premium Wash. 500 pts = Free Full Detail. Customers redeem directly in the app with one tap. You control the thresholds and the rewards.

Your customers see their points, rewards, and redemption options right in the app
